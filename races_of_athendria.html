Athendria's Magical Races

  • Humans

    Physically diverse, adaptable, and ambitious with varying tastes, morals, and customs.

  • Elves

    Includes Arboreal Elves and Dark Elves (Drow), known for their unearthly grace and connection to nature or the Underdark.


  • Dwarves

    Subcategories include Mountain Dwarves, Hill Dwarves, and Deep Dwarves.

  • Orcs

    Kamui Orcs (known for smithing) and Ebrean Orcs (located in southern Ebrea).

  • Goblinoids

    Bluebrows, Cinderskins, and Shrubbin Goblins.


    Hobgoblins and Bugbears.

  • Stoutlings

    Consists of Gnomes, Lightfoots, and Strongarms.

  • Uryons (The Warforged)

    Divided into Infrastructure Designation, Combat Designation, and Service Designation.

  • Dragonborn

    Categorized into Chromatic Dragonborn, Metallic Dragonborn, and Gemstone Dragonborn.



  • Giantfolk

    Includes Firbolgs, Oxenfolk, and Half-Giants.



  • Reptilia

    Comprising Turtlefolk, Lizardfolk, Snakefolk, Grung and Kobolds.

  • Pantheri

    Includes Catfolk and Lionfolk.

  • Birdfolk

    Consists of Skybound (Aarakokra) and Striders (Owlin, Kenku).

  • Yuan=Ti

    Snake-like abominations, or lithe, stealthy purebloods.

  • Aquaan

    Features Tritons, Merfolk, and Octarians.

  • Shifters

    Includes Swiftstride, Longtooth, and Beasthide.

  • Fae-Touched

    Comprises Changelings, Satyrs, and Eladrin.

  • Ascendants

    A progenitor race to many of Athendria's races.

  • Half-Breeds

    Hybrid races formed from pairings of various races.

  • Plane-touched

    Races with extraplanar influences, including Blightbloods (Infernal and Demonic Bloodlines).


    Angelborn (Protector, Fallen, Scourge)


    Elemental Lineages (Fire, Earth, Water, Air-blessed)
